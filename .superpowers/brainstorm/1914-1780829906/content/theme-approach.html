How should we handle the dark/light theme?

The site currently defaults to dark with a theme picker on first visit. The Artisan Market direction leans warm and light. Here are three approaches.

A

Light-first, dark optional

Warm cream is the default. Users can still switch to dark mode via a small toggle in the nav. The artisan feel hits immediately on load. The theme picker overlay goes away.

B

Light only — remove dark mode entirely

One experience, done beautifully. The warm cream artisan aesthetic everywhere. No toggle, no picker, no split decisions. Simpler to maintain and design. Most craft/food brands do this.

C

Keep dark default, but warm it up

The dark #0e0e0e background stays, but we inject the artisan feel through warmer accent colours, more hand-drawn type accents, texture overlays, and earthier tones. The light theme gets the full artisan treatment too. Best of both.
